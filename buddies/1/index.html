  • Male
  • Short hair

Atlas is a beautiful solid gray male cat with green eyes. He has been at Buddy since he was a kitten in need of medical assistance. This caused him to become shy and wary of people. With the help of volunteers he has become more trusting. Atlas will approach to be petted and loves to be brushed. He has bonded with other shy cats, both kittens and older cats, in the room. Atlas deserves a loving home with patient folks who will allow him to continue to blossom.

Atlas is one of our special buddies. He deserves a loving home, but may require more love and care.
